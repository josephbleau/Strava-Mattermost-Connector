Strava Mattermost Connector

Configuration: Step 1 (of 3)

Automatically post your new activities to your favorite Mattermost chat channel.

This application will ask you to authorize it with Strava and provide the following permissions:

  • (Required) View data about your public profile
  • (Optional) View data about your private activities

Note: Authorizing the application to view data about your private activities will allow it to read activities you have designated as visible to only you.
